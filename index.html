sorter for Eternal City characters!

pick your sources, and hit the start button

certain options have details that you can hover to read

depending on how many sources you pick, you'll get up to 200+ picks




keyboard controls during sorting

H/LeftArrow (pick left) J/DownArrow (undo) K/UpArrow (tie) L/RightArrow (pick right) S (save progress)


before sorting

S/Enter (start sorting) L (load progress)

1/2/3 always correspond to the first/second/third buttons





adapted from ojsort which is adapted from tohosort
